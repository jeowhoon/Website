Basic Vocabularies of Taiwanese Hokkien

Personal Pronouns

Hokkien English Translation Examples
我
guá		 “I” 我合汝無合伊來往。
You and I are not close to him.
阮
guán/gún		 Exclusive “we”
Also used as genetive “my/our”		 阮臺灣儂。
We Taiwanese.

阮老爸。
My/Our dad.
㑑
lán		 Inclusive “we”
Also used as genetive “our”		 恁是作武官，阮是作文官，㑑平平是政府其官吏。
You are military officers and we are civilian officials, but we are equally officialdom of the government.

㑑戍裏也有。
We have it in our home, too.
汝
lí		 Singular “you” 汝各飯坩，我也各碗籃。
You are to rice pot as I am to dish rack, meaning we’re roughly the same.
恁
lín		 Plural “you” 叫恁去。
Ask you all to go.
伊
i		 “He/she” 伊講怎樣？
What did he/she say?
〓
in		 “They” 〓大家。
All of them.
啥儂
siánn lâng, siáng/siâng		 “Who” 啥儂講？
Who said?

Demonstratives

Hokkien English Translation Examples
這
tse		 “This” 這是書。
This is a book.
這～
tsit—		 “This—” 這時陣。
This moment.
彼
he		 “That” 彼是一個不止仔熱其六月尾。
That was a very hot June.
彼～
hit—		 “That—” 彼膨鬆膨鬆其茶色頭鬃。
That fluffy brown hair.
這寡、遮其
tsit kuá, tsia ê		 “These” 這寡/遮其受苦其（靈魂）。
These (souls) that suffered.
彼寡、遐其
hit kuá, hia ê		 “Those” 彼寡/遐其有食魚仔其（儂）。
Those (people) who ate fish.
底～
tó—		 “Which—” 底蜀个老師好？
Which teacher is good?
啥、啥物
siánn, siánn mih		 “What” 問伊作啥/啥物（事業）。
Ask him what (business) he works for.
遮
tsia		 “Here”1 按遮來。
Start from here.
遐
hia		 “There”1 遮各遐有偌遠？
How far is it from here to there?
底、底位
tó, tó uī		 “Where”1 汝是欲去底/底位？
Where are you going?

Indefinite

Hokkien English Translation Examples
無…
bô…		 “No…” 無錢。
No money.
所有…
sóo ū…		 “Every…” 所有觀光區域。
Every tourist area.
有…
ū…		 “Some…” 有儂。
Someone.
任何…
jīm hô…		 “Any…” 任何表示。
Any indication.
別…
pát…		 “Other…” 別間公司。
Another company.

Interrogatives

Hokkien English translation Examples
當時、底時
tang sî, tī sî		 “When”2 㑑當時/底時才行解各彼个湖？
When will we get to that lake?
焉怎
án tsuánn		 “How, in what way”
偌…
jua…/gua…/lua…		 “How…, to what extend”
爲啥、是焉怎
uī siánn, sī án tsuánn		 “Why”
曷、曷使、曷著
áh, áh sái, áh tióh		 “Why should”
敢…
kám…		 “Whether…”

Suffix

Hokkien English translation
…其
…ê		 Genetive form “-’s”
…家己
…ka kī/tī		 Reflexive form “-self”

References

  1. Governor-General of Taiwan. (1931, March 10). Taiwanese-Japanese Dictionary. Retrieved from http://taigi.fhl.net/dict/index.html
  1. 「遮」,「遐」, and「底、底位」are actually corresponding to “this/that/what place” in English, as “here/there/where” are adverbs that mean “at this/that/what place.” [return]
  2. 「當時、底時」are actually corresponding to “what place” in English, as “when” is an adverb that means “at what time.” [return]
