Basic Vocabularies of Taiwanese Hokkien

Pronouns

Personal

Hokkien English Translation
我
guá		 “I”
阮
guán/gún		 Exclusive “we”—us, but not you.
Also used as genetive, “my,” “ours”
㑑
lán		 Inclusive “we”—you, me, and possibly others.
Also used as genetive, “ours”
汝
lí		 Singular “You”
恁
lín		 Plural “You”—all of you
伊
i		 “He/she”
𪜶
in		 “They”
啥儂
siánn lâng, siáng/siâng		 “Who”

Demonstrative

Hokkien English Translation
這
tse		 “This”
這寡、遮其
tsit kuá, tsia ê		 “These”
彼
he		 “That”
彼寡、遐其
hit kuá, hia ê		 “Those”
啥、啥物、啥貨
siánn, siánn mih, siánn huè/hè		 “What”
遮
tsia		 “Here”1
遐
hia		 “There”1
底、底位
tó, tó uī		 “Where”1

Interrogatives

Hokkien English translation
當時、底時
tang sî, tī sî		 “When”2
焉怎
án tsuánn		 “How”—in what way
偌…
jua…/gua…/lua…		 “How…”—to what extend
爲啥、是焉怎
uī siánn, sī án tsuánn		 “Why”
曷、曷使、曷著
áh, áh sái, áh tióh		 “Why should”
敢…
kám…		 “Whether…”

Suffix

Hokkien English translation
…其
…ê		 Genetive form “-’s”
…家己
…ka kī/tī		 Reflexive form “-self”
  1. 「遮」,「遐」, and「底、底位」are actually corresponding to “this/that/what place” in English as “here/there/where” are adverbs that mean “at this/that/what place.” [return]
  2. 「當時、底時」are actually corresponding to “what place” in English as “when” is an adverb that means “at what time” [return]
